nsbm

NSBM, which stands for National Socialist Black Metal, is a subgenre of black metal that incorporates far-right and neo-Nazi ideologies into its lyrics and imagery. The themes often revolve around white nationalism, anti-Semitism, racism, and fascism. This has resulted in NSBM being widely criticized and condemned by the metal community, as well as society at large, for promoting hateful and harmful ideas. It is important to note that hate speech and bigotry have no place in any form of art or expression.
